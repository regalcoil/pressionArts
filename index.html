This website aims to rebel against private ownership, specifically that of the elite and those others whose sole interest in art lies in the promise of future financial gains and current social status. The works are not credited and sources are avoided in order to showcase to aspiring artists that attribution for personal reward in the forms of fame, money, respect and sex is unnecessary and, rather, that giving the audience the gift of aesthetic appeal is the only thing worth pursuing as a creative.

curated as an extension of econometrics research analyzing the relationship between internet availability and art museum attendance
